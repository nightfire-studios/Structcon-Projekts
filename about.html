About Structcon Projekts

Structcon Projekts is a Ghanaian-owned limited liability firm based in Ghana’s capital city, Accra. We specialize in delivering top-tier engineering services and expertly managed construction project.

Our company’s genesis stems from the visionary minds of two accomplished structural engineers. Their goal was to fuse the realms of structural design and construction management into a unified entity, which we proudly call Design- Build.

This innovative approach simplifies the client experience by offering a single point of contact for both design and construction services, ensuring efficiency and cohesion throughout the project journey. At Structcon Projekts, we are dedicated to exceeding industry standards and client expectations. Our commitment to excellence, coupled with a deep pool of experienced professionals, ensures that every project we undertake is executed with precision and quality.

Structcon projekts limited commenced its operations in 2021 and subsequently achieved official registration as a limited liability company in 2022, headquartered in Accra,Ghana.

Our primary mission is to consistently deliver projects of superior quality, adhering to global standards while surpasing our clients' expectations.

Aiming to be the leading design-build company, our core objective is to provide dependable and high-quality construction services. We excel as a single point of contact for clients, seamiessly integrating design and construction to ensure comprehensive and efficient solutions.
